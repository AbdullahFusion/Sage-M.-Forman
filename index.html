About The Book

In Somewhere Between 'JUST FINE' and the Emergency Room, first-time author Sage M. Forman delves into the raw and intimate narrative of their journey through bipolar, grief, gender dysphoria, uncontrollably falling in love, and self-discovery.

About The Author

Somewhere Between Just Fine and the Emergency Room

Sage M. Forman is a first-time author living in the Midwest with their husband, two cats, and a young pit bull. After finishing art school, Forman returned home and began working in childcare, and as a doula for the LGBTQA+ community. Their interests include writing, short films, and gardening.
